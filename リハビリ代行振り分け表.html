Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 11, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how "Rehabilitation Assignment App" ("we", "our", or "app") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

Our app is designed to work locally on your device and does not collect or transmit personal information to external servers. The app collects and stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Staff information (names, PHS numbers, availability status) that you input
  • Patient information (names, assignment status, admission dates) that you input
  • Assignment data for rehabilitation scheduling

2. How We Use Your Information

The information stored in the app is used solely for the purpose of:

  • Managing rehabilitation staff assignments
  • Coordinating patient scheduling
  • Facilitating substitute staff arrangements
  • Providing you with the core functionality of the app

3. Data Storage and Protection

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults system. The data does not leave your device unless you explicitly use iOS system features to share or backup your device.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. The app does not have any server-side components and does not transmit data over the internet.

5. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you maintain complete control over it. You can delete all app data by:

  • Using the reset function within the app's settings
  • Uninstalling the application from your device

6. Children's Privacy

The app is designed for professional use in medical environments and is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age.

7. Analytics and Crash Reporting

The app does not use any third-party analytics or crash reporting services.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

9. App Permissions

The app does not request any special permissions from your device.

This app complies with Apple's App Store Review Guidelines regarding privacy and data collection. The app:
