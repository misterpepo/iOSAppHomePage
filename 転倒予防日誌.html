Fall Prevention Journal - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 11, 2025

The Fall Prevention Journal app ("our app") respects and protects the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Our app may collect the following information:

  • User-Provided Information: Name, age, and other profile information you provide
  • Health-Related Data: Symptom records, exercise logs, fall risk assessments, and other data you enter into the app
  • Usage Data: How frequently you use the app and which features you utilize
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and other technical information necessary for troubleshooting

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide app functionality (fall prevention journaling, exercise tracking, etc.)
  • To improve and customize the app experience
  • To diagnose and resolve technical issues
  • To develop new features and improvements

3. Data Storage

Data collected by our app is stored as follows:

  • On-Device Storage: Health data and user profiles are primarily stored on your device and are not transmitted to external servers
  • iCloud Backup: If you have iCloud backup enabled, app data may be backed up through Apple's iCloud service

4. Information Sharing

We do not share your information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • When required to comply with legal obligations
  • When necessary for service providers who support app functionality (such as analytics tools)
  • With your explicit consent

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, please understand that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. HealthKit Integration

Our app may integrate with Apple's HealthKit framework. Any data acquired from HealthKit is used solely for providing app functionality and is never sold, used, or disclosed to third parties.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app does not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13. If a child under 13 uses our app, they should do so under parental supervision.

8. Your Rights

As a user, you have the following rights:

  • The right to access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • The right to withdraw consent for data processing
  • The right to inquire about privacy concerns

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page or through an in-app notification. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

10. Disclaimer

Our app is designed to support fall prevention and health management but is not a substitute for medical advice or diagnosis. Always consult with healthcare professionals before making health-related decisions.
