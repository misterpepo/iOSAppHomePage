Privacy Policy

Walking Analysis App

Your Privacy Matters: This app is designed with privacy in mind. All data processing occurs locally on your device, and no personal information is transmitted to external servers.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Sensor Data

Our app collects the following sensor data from your device to perform walking analysis:

  • Accelerometer Data: Used to measure body movements and calculate gait stability metrics
  • Step Count Data: Used to count steps during walking tests
  • Camera Data (AR Mode): Used exclusively for distance measurement via ARKit technology

1.2 Measurement Results

The app stores analysis results including:

  • Walking speed calculations
  • Body sway measurements (RMS values)
  • Step count and timing data
  • User-provided measurement titles and timestamps

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Primary Purpose

All collected data is used exclusively for:

  • Performing real-time walking analysis
  • Calculating gait stability metrics
  • Displaying measurement results
  • Allowing you to save and compare measurements

2.2 Local Processing

All data processing occurs entirely on your device. The app does not:

  • Send data to external servers
  • Share information with third parties
  • Use cloud storage services
  • Perform any network-based analytics

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All measurement data is stored locally on your device using:

  • iOS secure storage mechanisms
  • App-specific data containers
  • No external database connections

3.2 Data Retention

Measurement data is retained until:

  • You manually delete individual measurements
  • You uninstall the application
  • You reset your device (data will be permanently deleted)

3.3 Data Limits

The app automatically manages storage by limiting saved measurements to 50 entries, removing the oldest entries when this limit is exceeded.

4. Data Sharing and Export

4.1 User-Controlled Export

You can export your data via:

  • CSV file export using iOS sharing functionality
  • Text copying of individual measurement results

4.2 No Automatic Sharing

The app does not automatically share data with any external services, analytics platforms, or third-party applications.

5. Permissions and Access

5.1 Required Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Motion & Fitness: To access accelerometer and step counting data
  • Camera (AR Mode only): To perform distance measurements using ARKit

5.2 Permission Management

You can manage these permissions through your device's Settings app. Denying permissions may limit app functionality but will not affect your privacy.

6. Bluetooth Connectivity

6.1 Local Device Communication

When using BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy) mode:

  • Data is only transmitted between your paired devices
  • No data is sent over the internet
  • Communication is encrypted using standard Bluetooth security
  • Only measurement data is shared between devices

7. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed for general health and fitness analysis and does not require personal identification.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify users of any material changes through app updates.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data stored by the app
  • Delete individual measurements or all data
  • Export your data in CSV format
  • Disable specific app permissions
  • Uninstall the app to permanently delete all data

10. Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is stored using iOS secure storage APIs
  • No network transmission of personal data
  • App sandbox isolation from other applications
  • Regular security updates through App Store
